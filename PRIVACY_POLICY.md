# Privacy Policy for Learn GCS

Last updated: February 26, 2026

## Overview
Learn GCS is an educational app for practicing Glasgow Coma Scale scoring.

## Information We Collect
Learn GCS does not collect personal information.

## Data Stored on Device
The app stores the following data locally on your device:
- Quiz progress (remaining questions)
- Quiz performance stats (such as total correct and streak)

This data is used only to support app functionality and is not transmitted to remote servers.

## Third-Party Services
This version of Learn GCS does not include advertising SDKs and does not use analytics tracking for personal profiling.

## Children's Privacy
Learn GCS is an educational app and does not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## Your Choices
You can reset your quiz progress by starting a new quiz session in the app.
